### [Organic Matter](#om-testing)
      
The  median OM measurements can be summarised as follows: 

- Comparing the **GREEN** samples taken on September 18th, 2024 to their most recent previous samples (October 11th, 2023).
  * At a depth of **0-2 cm**, the OM content increased from 9.32% to 9.98%. 
  * At a depth of **2-4 cm**, the OM content increased from 3.68% to 4.46%. 
  * At a depth of **4-6 cm**, the OM content increased from 1.33% to 1.34%.
- Comparing the **ROUGH** samples taken on October 10th, 2022 to their most recent previous samples (September 27th, 2021).
  * At a depth of **0-2 cm**, the OM content increased from 20.28% to 21.09%. 
  * At a depth of **2-4 cm**, the OM content increased from 13.4% to 13.73%. 
  * At a depth of **4-6 cm**, the OM content increased from 6.65% to 8.99%.
- Comparing the **TEE** samples taken on September 18th, 2024 to their most recent previous samples (October 11th, 2023).
  * At a depth of **0-2 cm**, the OM content increased from 8.26% to 9.79%. 
  * At a depth of **2-4 cm**, the OM content decreased from 4.6% to 4.39%. 
  * At a depth of **4-6 cm**, the OM content decreased from 4.42% to 3.81%.

*The results show median values.
The median value is the central point of the data, meaning half of the numbers in a list are smaller and half are larger than the median.*
The OM Accumulation Rate was calculated for GREEN and TEE samples.

* GREEN
  - At a depth of 2 cm, from 11th October 2023 to 18th September 2024 the OM content increased from 8.83% to 9.36%. This represents an OM Accumulation Rate of 16.72 g per kg of soil per year.
* TEE
  - At a depth of 2 cm, from 11th October 2023 to 18th September 2024 the OM content increased from 7.01% to 7.96%. This represents an OM Accumulation Rate of 32.54 g per kg of soil per year.
The Sand Requirement, based on the OM Accumulation Rate was calculated for GREEN and TEE samples.

* GREEN
  - Adding **2 mm** of sand to **2 cm** depth by September 18th, 2025 will result in a total organic matter content of **9.36%** if the OM accumulation rate remains at 16.72 g/kg
* TEE
  - Adding **4 mm** of sand to **2 cm** depth by September 18th, 2025 will result in a total organic matter content of **7.96%** if the OM accumulation rate remains at 32.54 g/kg
